Home-based Parcel Forwarding Coordinator
Position:
Remote/Home-based
Location:
We are looking for dedicated, home-based individuals, such as stay-at-home parents to join our team as Parcel Forwarding Coordinators. The role involves receiving parcels from delivery services and forwarding them accordingly, ensuring a critical part of the supply chain operates smoothly.
Job Overview:
While the job may seem straightforward, it requires a high level of responsibility and attentiveness.
Responsibility and Attention to Details:
  • Receive parcels from delivery companies such as DHL, UPS, etc., ensuring they are securely received without damage.
  • Forward parcels to specified addresses as per the instructions provided.
  • Maintain accurate records of all parcels using our proprietary software system.
  • Communicate effectively with your supervisor to report any issues or to receive further instructions.
  • Be available at home to accept deliveries during standard delivery hours.
Key Responsibilities:
  • Must have a home environment suitable for securely receiving parcels throughout the day.
  • Strong organizational skills with a keen eye for detail.
  • Basic computer proficiency and a willingness to learn and use company software.
  • Have a basic knowledge of the language of the country you are in.
  • Capable of lifting and handling parcels of various sizes.
  • Previous experience in logistics or a similar field is beneficial but not necessary.
Requirements:
  • Pay: $10-30 per parcel.
  • Contract: We offer long-term contracts with all necessary legal documentation to formalize our relationships.

You will earn $10-30 for each processed package. We also cover all related expenses like shipping costs and packaging materials. On average, processing 50-70 packages per month can result in earnings of around $1000+. Payments are made through bank transfer, PayPal. During your first month, payments are issued weekly, and starting from the second month, they are made bi-monthly.
No, you won’t have to cover any costs during the hiring process or while working. All necessary work-related expenses are covered by our company, and at no point will you be asked to make any financial contributions.
You’ll handle a variety of goods, ranging from clothing and cosmetics to electronics. Electronics are often the most common items due to their popularity with clients. You are allowed to open packages to check and photograph the contents, which is often requested by clients for order verification.
Many U.S. online stores do not ship directly to Eastern European countries. Our company provides clients with virtual addresses in countries where these stores do deliver. Our team receives the packages at these addresses and forwards them to clients mostly in Eastern Europe, making it possible for them to access goods from U.S. stores.
The number of packages varies by region, but typically, you’ll handle around 50 to 70 packages per month. This workload allows us to optimize efficiency while ensuring you can manage your tasks effectively.
Most packages will be delivered directly to your home by courier services like DHL, FedEx, or UPS. In some cases, you may need to pick up packages from local pickup points or lockers, which we will arrange to be as convenient as possible for you.
Sending packages is straightforward and depends on the courier service used. You’ll need to visit the local branch of the courier service to dispatch the package. If you’re unfamiliar with the process, we’ll guide you through it. Sometimes, you’ll receive prepaid labels to affix to packages, and in other cases, we may transfer funds to cover shipping costs in advance.

Deadlines may vary depending on circumstances like receiving client instructions or requests for shipment delays. Generally, once you receive a notification to send a package, we expect it to be dispatched within two days to maintain our commitment to fast service.
You’ll receive packages from a wide array of stores, including major online platforms like Amazon and eBay, as well as specialty retailers like Victoria’s Secret, Farfetch, and Rituals. This variety ensures your work remains dynamic and engaging.
The job offers a flexible schedule, allowing you to coordinate delivery times with courier services.
